Changi Airport Line

Developer(s)

willis_chong   SMRTO Studios

Description

The Changi Airport Line (Extension) is one of the few train lines in Singapore, an extension of the East-West Line. The route is made by willis_chong. Upgrades are done in-house by SMRTO Studios.

Recommended OpenBVE Version: 1.9.27 (64-bit)
